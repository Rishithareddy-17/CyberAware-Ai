The Problem: Human Error in Cybersecurity

Clicking on phishing links, using weak passwords, and unsafe data sharing cause most breaches. 82% of breaches involve human error (Verizon Report 2024).

Phishing

Deceptive emails and messages trick users into revealing sensitive data.

Weak Passwords

Simple, reused passwords make accounts easy targets.

Social Engineering

Attackers manipulate people into breaking normal security protocols.

“The weakest link in cybersecurity is human error — and the strongest defense is awareness.”
